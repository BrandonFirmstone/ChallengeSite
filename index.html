North Wales has an amazing variety of landscapes to visit and explore. From medieval castles to towns with ancient legends, there are so many brilliant places to see.

Some of the most breath-taking views in the UK are in North Wales. From enormous mountains, beautiful beaches and unique villages, North Wales has a lot to offer.

The Tallest Mountain in Wales, Snowdon

Snowdon, or Yr Wyddfa in Welsh, is not only the highest mountain in Wales, but the Snowdonia Mountain Range is host to a plethora of fascinating places.

Being one of Wales' most famous points, the mountain has a railway that takes you straight to the summit as well as a cafe right at the top.

The view from the top is phenomenal, you can see the entire area surrounding the mountain. You can see the Snowdonia area, Llanberis, Anglesey, Pembrokeshire and even Ireland!
